## Spoken-word constraints (non-negotiable, every pack)

- Written to be read aloud on camera: breath-group line breaks, one idea per breath.
- No clause stacking — if a sentence needs a comma AND a "which," split it.
- Numbers rounded the way people say them ("almost half" beats "47.3%" — exact figure goes in the overlay).
- Delivery markers welcome: *(beat)*, *(faster)*, **emphasis**.
