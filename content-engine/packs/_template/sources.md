## Tier rubric (the vetting layer applies this to EVERY catch — see engine/source-vetting.md)

Tiers judge the **origin** of a claim, not whoever wrote it up.

- **Tier 1 — Primary/authoritative.** {For science: peer-reviewed journals, government agencies, major research institutions. For local news: the official announcement, the government filing, the venue itself. Adapt per topic.} Verified legitimate. Can support any factual claim.
- **Tier 2 — Quality journalism.** Bylined outlets with editorial standards that cite sources and whose Tier 1 origin is traceable. Trace and cite the origin.
- **Tier 3 — Trend/culture.** Trend pieces, brand surveys, social platforms, influencer claims. Can ONLY support "this is trending" framing — never a factual claim alone. Best place for STORY IDEAS; receipts come from Tiers 1–2.
