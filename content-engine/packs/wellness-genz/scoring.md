# Scoring config: Wellness × Gen Z

| Dimension | Weight | This pack's interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Audience fit | 0.30 | Does it touch a daily Gen Z reality (sleep, stress, scrolling, energy, friendship)? Abstract policy/science with no personal stake scores low. |
| Source strength | 0.25 | Health claims demand real tiers. Human studies > animal/in-vitro. RCTs and meta-analyses top the list. |
| Freshness | 0.15 | Wellness news decays in ~72h; an older study only works with a "this resurfaced and here's the truth" angle. |
| Hook potential | 0.20 | Counterintuitive findings and trend reality-checks are the strongest hooks for this audience. "Confirms what you suspected" also works. |
| Mission fit | 0.10 | Usable + constructive. Pure fear ("your phone is destroying you") fails even when sourced. |

## Topic-specific boosts/penalties
- +1 if the viewer can act on it today (or stop worrying about something today)
- +1 if it reality-checks something currently trending on TikTok
- −2 fear-bait framing, even if accurate
- −2 "in mice" studies presented as human findings (cover only WITH the caveat as the angle)
- Auto-drop: supplement-brand-funded studies without independent work; duty-of-care topics per pack.md
