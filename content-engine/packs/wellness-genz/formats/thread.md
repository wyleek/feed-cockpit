# Thread Format — wellness-genz (Twitter/X)

A 5–8 tweet thread. Goal: unpack one health/wellness finding in a way that gets saved and shared.

## Structure

**Tweet 1 — Hook** (this is the only one most people read)
- Bold claim or surprising stat from the story
- Max 240 chars
- End with: 🧵

**Tweets 2–6 — The breakdown**
- Each tweet = one complete thought
- Build logically: what → why → so what
- Short paragraphs — 2–3 lines max per tweet
- Use line breaks for visual breathing room

**Tweet 7 — The practical takeaway**
- "Here's what to actually do:" opener
- 2–3 bullet points using em dashes or "→"
- Concrete, specific, actionable

**Tweet 8 — Close + CTA**
- Reframe the whole thread in 1 sentence
- Ask a question or invite a share
- No "RT if you agree" — aim for genuine engagement prompts

## Style rules
- No hashtags in thread body — one at the very end if warranted
- Numbers and stats beat vague claims ("37% of..." not "many people...")
- Keep expert-curious tone: smart but not academic
- Emoji: optional, 1 per tweet max
- Each tweet must work standalone (no "as I said above")
