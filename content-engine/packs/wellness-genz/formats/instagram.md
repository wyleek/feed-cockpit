# Instagram Carousel Format — wellness-genz

5–7 slide carousel post. Each slide = one visual frame worth of copy.

## Slide structure

**Slide 1 — Hook**
- Bold statement or question (max 8 words)
- Subtext line: the tease (max 12 words)
- "swipe →" CTA

**Slides 2–5 — Content slides**
- Headline: short label (3–5 words)
- Body: 1–3 punchy lines — conversational, no filler
- Each slide = one complete idea

**Slide 6 — The takeaway**
- "The bottom line:" header
- 1–2 sentence summary
- Practical action if relevant

**Slide 7 (optional) — CTA**
- Save / share / follow prompt
- Question to drive comments (max 10 words)

## Copy rules
- Max 40 words per slide (aim for 20–30)
- No bullet points in slide copy — write it like a caption
- Bold key phrases in brackets: [bold this]
- Emoji: 1 per slide max, only if it adds meaning
- Avoid "I" framing — write for the reader's experience ("you", "we", or neutral)
