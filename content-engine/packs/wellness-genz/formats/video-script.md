# Format: 30–45 second video script

## Beat sheet (the skeleton — stable across every video)

1. **HOOK** (0:00–0:02) — one line, ≤10 words spoken. No greeting, no setup.
2. **STAKES** (0:02–0:08) — why the viewer should care, in their terms.
3. **THE STORY** (0:08–0:28) — what happened / what was found. The specific detail lives here.
4. **THE RECEIPT** (0:28–0:35) — name the source out loud. Citation overlay on screen NOW.
5. **BUTTON** (0:35–0:45) — the takeaway + signature closer per the voice bible.

## Budgets
- 75–115 words total (≈2.5 words/sec). State estimated read time at the top of every script.
- Hook ≤10 words. Button ≤20 words.

## Page format
Scripts are delivered in breath-group lines (one spoken phrase per line), with delivery markers inline. The creator reads this off a teleprompter app — format for the eye at arm's length.
