# Breakdown Format — wellness-genz

A concise topic explainer structured for short-form video or a stitched series.
Goal: make a complex health/wellness topic feel approachable and interesting to Gen Z.

## Structure

**Hook line** (1 sentence — the surprising or counterintuitive part)

**The Basics** (2–3 sentences — what is this thing, plain language, no jargon)

**Why it matters for you** (2–3 sentences — connect to Gen Z daily life: sleep, school, work, social)

**The research says** (1–2 sentences — cite the finding from the story without being clinical)

**What to actually do** (2–3 bullet points — practical, specific, not preachy)

**Closing reframe** (1 sentence — leaves them with a new mental model)

## Tone
- Curious teacher, not lecturer
- Short sentences — conversational, punchy
- Avoid: "It's important to...", "You should...", "Studies show..."
- Use: "Here's the wild part...", "Basically...", "Real talk:"
