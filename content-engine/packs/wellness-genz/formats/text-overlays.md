# Text Overlays Format — wellness-genz

On-screen text for a short-form video (TikTok / Reels / Shorts).
These appear as timed text over the video — not captions/subtitles, but editorial overlays.

## Output format

Deliver as a numbered list of overlays in display order.

Each overlay:
```
[TIMING] TEXT
```

Where TIMING is: opening / 5s / 10s / mid / final — approximate cue point.

## Structure

**Opening overlay** — appears in first 2 seconds, hooks the scroll
- Max 6 words
- Should standalone as a question, stat, or provocative statement

**Body overlays** (3–5) — reinforce the spoken/visual content
- Highlight a key phrase from the script
- Add context the viewer needs without stopping to read a paragraph
- Max 8 words each

**Punchline overlay** — appears near the end
- The "aha" or reframe
- Max 8 words

**CTA overlay** — final screen
- "follow for more", "save this", or a question
- Max 8 words

## Rules
- CAPS for emphasis sparingly (1–2 overlays max)
- No full sentences with periods — fragments feel native to the format
- Don't duplicate what the voiceover says — complement it
- Emoji at the end of an overlay: OK if earned
